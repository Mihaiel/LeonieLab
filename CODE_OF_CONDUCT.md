# Code of Conduct

## Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in LeonieLab a
respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the users, students with motor impairments,
  the project community and especially user accessibility
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, trolling, or insulting comments in any form
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported by
opening a private issue or contacting the project maintainer directly via GitHub.
All reports will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response deemed
appropriate to the circumstances.
